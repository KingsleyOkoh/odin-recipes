Slow-Cooked Pepper Steak

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Steak
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients: In a small bowl combine all the sauce ingredients together and set aside.
  2. Cook the Steak: Add 1 tbsp of the vegetable oil to a skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
